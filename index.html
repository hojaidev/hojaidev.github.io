Eletraces Mobile App

Version 1.6

ELETRACES is an Android based GPS enabled mobile app to track the GPS location of the movement of the vehicle carrying EVM before poll day and on poll day. All Zonal Officers, Sector Officers and Presiding Officers involved in the Assam Assembly Election 2021 will have to use this app during transportation of EVM before poll day and on poll day. For more info, please refer to the manual and SOP given below.
